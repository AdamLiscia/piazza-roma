Meet Our Team

Chef Michael Thorne

Executive Chef

Trained in Paris and New York, bringing over 15 years of culinary expertise to Piazza Roma. Chef Thorne's innovative approach combines classical French techniques with modern American flavors. His signature dish, the herb-crusted rack of lamb, has become legendary among our regular patrons.

Certified by the Court of Master Sommeliers, Brian brings passion and expertise to our wine program. His travels through Europe's wine regions have shaped our carefully curated wine list. He takes pride in finding the perfect pairing for each guest's unique palate.
