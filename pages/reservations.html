Reservation Policies

• Reservations are required for parties of 6 or more, with 24-hour advance notice

• We hold reserved tables for 15 minutes past the reservation time

• For parties of 8 or more, we require a credit card to secure your reservation

• Special events, holiday reservations, and large group bookings may require deposits

• Cancellations for large parties must be made 24 hours in advance to avoid charges

• Private dining rooms are available with minimum spend requirements

• Special dietary requirements can be accommodated with advance notice

Welcome to Piazza Roma

Step into the warm and inviting atmosphere of Piazza Roma, where we bring the heart of Italian hospitality to Mohegan Lake. Our intimate dining spaces are thoughtfully designed to create the perfect setting for every occasion, from romantic dinners to family celebrations and business gatherings.

We offer several distinct dining areas to suit your preferences: our main dining room features elegant décor and soft lighting for a sophisticated dining experience, while our cozy wine room provides an intimate setting surrounded by our curated wine collection. During warmer months, our charming outdoor terrace captures the essence of al fresco dining in Italy, complete with gentle breezes and stunning sunset views.

For special events and larger gatherings, our private dining room accommodates up to 40 guests, providing a sophisticated space for celebrations and corporate functions. Each space has been carefully designed to ensure comfort while maintaining the perfect ambiance for conversation and culinary enjoyment.

To ensure the best possible dining experience, we recommend making reservations, especially for weekend dining and special occasions. Our dedicated staff is committed to making your visit memorable, from the moment you book your table to the final bite of our house-made desserts. For parties of six or more, please book at least 24 hours in advance to allow us to prepare for your group properly.
