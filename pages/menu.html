Our Culinary Philosophy

At Piazza Roma, we honor the time-honored traditions of Italian cuisine while embracing modern culinary innovations. Our executive chef brings over two decades of experience from renowned kitchens across Italy and America, crafting dishes that celebrate both heritage and innovation. Learn more about our culinary journey and commitment to authentic Italian dining.

Our menu changes seasonally to showcase the finest ingredients at their peak. We partner with local farmers and artisanal producers within a 100-mile radius to source the freshest vegetables, herbs, and dairy products. Select premium ingredients are imported directly from Italy, including our extra virgin olive oil from Tuscany, aged balsamic vinegar from Modena, and specialty cheeses from small-batch producers across various Italian regions.

From hand-rolled pasta made fresh daily to our wood-fired pizzas featuring naturally leavened dough, every element of our menu reflects our dedication to traditional Italian cooking methods. Our pasta is crafted using heritage grain flours, and our sauces simmer slowly, developing rich, complex flavors that transport you to the heart of Italy.

Our wine list, carefully curated by our in-house sommelier, features both celebrated Italian vintages and emerging producers, perfectly complementing our culinary offerings. With over 200 labels representing every major wine region of Italy, we offer perfect pairings for every dish and occasion. Join us for our regular wine tasting events to explore our collection.

For those with dietary preferences or restrictions, our kitchen team is happy to accommodate your needs. Many of our dishes can be adapted to vegetarian, vegan, or gluten-free preparations without compromising on flavor or authenticity. Please inform your server of any dietary requirements, and we'll ensure a memorable dining experience tailored to your needs.
